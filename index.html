Welcome To RC Flooring and Granite

RC Flooring & Granite has been one of Dallas's most trusted names in flooring supply and installation since 1988. We stock one of the largest selection of carpet, hardwood, luxury vinyl and other designer favorites in the area. From beautiful flooring to gorgeous quartz and granite, you can expect quality products and expert craftsmanship every time.

Why Choose Us?

With over 35 years of experience, we offer one of Dallas's largest selections of premium flooring and stone surfaces. Our expert team delivers top-quality craftsmanship, competitive pricing, and outstanding customer service. From selection to installation, we ensure beauty, durability, and style in every project.

Trusted Flooring Experts Since 1988

For over three decades, RC Flooring & Granite has been Dallas's go-to source for premium flooring and expert installation. Our commitment to quality and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted name in the industry.

Extensive Selection of Premium Flooring

Explore one of the largest collections of carpet, hardwood, luxury vinyl, and more. Whether you're looking for classic elegance or modern durability, we have the perfect flooring to match your style and needs.

Stunning Quartz & Granite Countertops

Quality Craftsmanship, Every Time

From selection to installation, our experienced team ensures top-tier craftsmanship and attention to detail. You can trust RC Flooring & Granite to bring your vision to life with precision and care.
